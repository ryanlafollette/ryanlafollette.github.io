ELIJAH MULLENAX

😝

Elijah Mullenax is an up and coming young artist, originally from Brevard, North Carolina, now living in Carrboro. He is an aspiring author and spends much of his time writing fiction. Elijah is currently working on his first book, a fantasy novel entitled Jimmy's Painted World. Elijah is also an avid musician. He enjoys writing and playing folk music on his guitar, as well as busking in the Chapel Hill/Carrboro area.
