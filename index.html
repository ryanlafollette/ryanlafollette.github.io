ELIJAH MULLENAX

Elijah Mullenax is an up and coming creator. Originally from Brevard, North Carolina, now living in Carrboro, he's an aspiring author and spends much of his time writing fiction. Elijah's currently working on his first book, Jimmy's Painted World, a coming of age story involving the combination of art and magic with unforseen consequences. When he's not writing stories, you can catch him writing and playing folk music on his guitar, busking in the Chapel Hill/Carrboro area.

Enjoy the site, and don't forget to stay creative!
